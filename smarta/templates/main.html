SMARTA is a smart object that, during a constructive conversation, is passed around between participants.
By lighting up in different colors, making sounds and vibrating, SMARTA signals the users on how the
conversation is going. The ball can also detect whether somebody is interrupting the current speaker
or if another participant is willing to talk next.
